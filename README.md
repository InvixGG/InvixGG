Hi there 👋 My name is Levi van Boxtel
================================

Company Owner, Software Developer & Video Editor
------------------------------------------------

I am a dynamic professional who, at the age of 20, has already carved out a seasoned career in the digital world. Over the past six years, I have taken the initiative to establish five businesses while also lending my expertise to four diverse companies.

My proficiency extends to the realm of video editing, where I have spent six years crafting professionally edited videos for more than 75 companies, tailoring each one to meet unique business requirements.

I have improved my skills as a game developer during my academic years at SintLucas Eindhoven, where I developed an in-depth understanding of the Unity Game Engine and the C# programming language. This knowledge has enabled me to design and create engaging games.

Adding to my technical skill set, I have successfully worked on a Building Information Modeling (BIM) Viewer with a comprehensive set of features. This project enabled me to demonstrate my versatility with several coding languages, including Vue.js, JavaScript, HTML, SCSS, and TypeScript.

In addition to my technical capabilities, I have developed robust skills in YouTube Marketing & Management. I have set up multiple YouTube channels, each time focusing on optimizing for the platform's algorithm and maximizing searchability. My expertise in this area ensures increased visibility and engagement, driving growth and reaching targeted audiences effectively.

*   🌍  I'm based in The Netherlands
